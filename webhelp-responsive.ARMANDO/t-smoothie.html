How to make a Smoothie

This recipe will allow you to make a delicious smoothie to start your day off right!

Ingredients/Tools Required:

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Almond Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil
  • 2 tablespoons Chia Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Powdered Ginger
  • Blender
  • Large Cup
1 serving of smoothie

Instructions

  1. Plug in the blender into the wall
  2. Slice up 1 banana
  3. Put 1 cup of almond milk, 1 tablespoon of coconut oil, 2 tablespoons of chia seeds, and 1 teaspoon of powdered ginger into the blender
  4. Put the blender lid cover on top of the machine and hit the blend button
    Mix thoroughly to make sure ingredients do not clump together
    Do not remove the lid or put your hand into the blender as it blends, you will lose a finger
  5. Pour mixture into a large cup and enjoy
  • Wash all used dishes and utensils with dishwasher soap
  • Clean all used bowls with warm water and dishwasher soap
  • Clean dirty counter tops with paper towels and water
  • Clean all used tools

If you do not know the details to properly and thoroughly clean dishes or the kitchen, please refer to How to Properly and Thoroughly Clean the Kitchen
